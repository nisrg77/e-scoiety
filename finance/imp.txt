## Proposed Changes: Finance App 
**Goal:** Track maintenance billing, invoices, and expense reports.

### finance/models.py
- `Invoice`: `resident` (FK to `ResidentProfile`), `month`, `year`, `amount`, `description` (e.g. "Monthly Maintenance"), `status` (`unpaid`, `paid`), `due_date`
- `Payment`: `invoice` (FK), `amount_paid`, `payment_date`, `transaction_id`
- `SocietyExpense`: `title`, `amount`, `date`, `category` (Maintenance, Salary, Electricity)

### finance/services.py
- `generate_monthly_invoices(year, month, amount)` (Admin)
- `get_resident_invoices(resident_id)`
- `record_payment(invoice_id, amount, transaction_id)`
- `generate_expense_report(start_date, end_date)` (Admin)

### finance/views.py & finance/urls.py
- `my_invoices_view`
- `pay_invoice_view`
- `expense_report_view`
