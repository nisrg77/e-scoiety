## Proposed Changes: Notifications App 
**Goal:** Bulletin board for announcements, polls, and discussions.

### notifications/models.py
- `Notice`: `title`, `content`, `created_by` (FK to `core.User`), `created_at`, `is_pinned`
- `Poll`: `question`, `created_by` (FK), `created_at`, `is_active`
- `PollOption`: `poll` (FK), `option_text`, `votes` (IntegerField)

### notifications/services.py
- `create_notice(user_id, title, content)`
- `get_active_notices()`
- `create_poll(user_id, question, options_list)`
- `cast_vote(resident_id, option_id)`

### notifications/views.py & notifications/urls.py
- `notice_board_view`
- `poll_list_view`
- `vote_view`
