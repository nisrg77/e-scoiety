## Proposed Changes: Complaints App

### complaints/models.py
Define the `Complaint` model to track issues raised by residents:
- `resident` (ForeignKey to `residents.ResidentProfile`, the reporter)
- `category` (CharField choices: `plumbing`, `electrical`, `security`, `noise`, `other`)
- `title` (CharField)
- `description` (TextField)
- `status` (CharField choices: `open`, `in_progress`, `resolved`, `closed`)
- `created_at` (DateTimeField)
- `updated_at` (DateTimeField)
- `resolution_remarks` (TextField, blank=True)

### complaints/services.py
Create business logic functions:
- `create_complaint(resident_id, data)`
- `update_complaint_status(complaint_id, status, remarks=None)`
- `get_resident_complaints(resident_id)`
- `get_all_complaints(status=None)` (For admin dashboard)

### complaints/views.py & complaints/urls.py
Implement views that utilize the services:
- **Resident**: `raise_complaint_view`, `my_complaints_view`
- **Admin**: `manage_complaints_view` (Update status)
