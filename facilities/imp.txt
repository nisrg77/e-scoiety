## Proposed Changes: Facilities App 
**Goal:** Enable shared amenity booking and track availability.

### facilities/models.py
- `Facility`: `name` (Gym, Pool, Hall), `description`, `capacity`, `booking_fee`
- `FacilityBooking`: `facility` (FK), `resident` (FK to `ResidentProfile`), `date`, `start_time`, `end_time`, `status` (`confirmed`, `cancelled`), `payment_status`

### facilities/services.py
- `list_facilities()`
- `check_availability(facility_id, date, start_time, end_time)`
- `book_facility(resident_id, facility_id, date, start_time, end_time)`

### facilities/views.py & facilities/urls.py
- `facility_list_view`
- `book_facility_view`
- `my_bookings_view`
