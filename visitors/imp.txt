## Proposed Changes: Visitors App

### visitors/models.py
Define the `Visitor` model to track who is entering the society:
- `resident` (ForeignKey to `residents.ResidentProfile`, the host)
- `name` (CharField)
- `phone` (CharField)
- `purpose` (CharField)
- `vehicle_number` (CharField, blank=True)
- `entry_time` (DateTimeField)
- `exit_time` (DateTimeField, blank=True, null=True)
- `status` (CharField choices: `pending`, `approved`, `rejected`, `entered`, `exited`)

### visitors/services.py
Create business logic functions:
- `create_visitor_request(resident_id, visitor_data)`
- `update_visitor_status(visitor_id, status)` (e.g., Security marks them as 'entered' or 'exited')
- `get_expected_visitors()` (For security dashboard)
- `get_resident_visitors(resident_id)` (For resident dashboard)

### visitors/views.py & visitors/urls.py
Implement views that utilize the services:
- **Resident**: `add_visitor_view`, `my_visitors_view`
- **Security**: `security_visitor_log_view` (Update status)
